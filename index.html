Dom The Penguin

Genre: Insturmental/math/post roc

Members: Dom (vocals), David (trumpet), Francis (guitar), Mike (Percusion)

Hometown: Mystic, CT

Record Label: Proud Penguin Records

Influences: Owen, Radiohead, American Football, Zach Babylon, Suzy Lyon, This Town Needs Guns, Matt Sbalcio

Bio: On December 25, 2012, Dominic T. Penguin was admitted to rehab for the sixth time in three years due to his unworldly alcoholism. Soon after being released, a photo of an obviously inebriated Dominic appeared on the cover of Rolling Stone Magazine. Upon refusing to return to rehab, Dominic's friends Frank and David approached the washed up celebrity and inquired as to if he would do them the honor of singing for their new musical project. Whether it was Dominic's fiery passion for art, his kindness, or his BAC at the time (an astounding .31), he agreed and Dom the Penguin was born in the early months of 2011. Frank and David originally planned to use the group as a means to get Dominic back on his feet, but they quickly realized their plan was futile. However, they have influenced Dominic's alcoholism in a positive manner since the band's inception. Today, Dominic only performs and records buzzed. Additionally, the one drink Dominic enjoys before the ripe hour of 10:00 AM is the classic Appletini.
